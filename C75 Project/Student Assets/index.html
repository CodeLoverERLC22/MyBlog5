School Church

Was established in 1901

St Joseph’s is a large suburban parish covering the districts of Wealdstone and Harrow Weald in the Archdiocese of Westminster and is located in the London Borough of Harrow. It was founded by the Salvatorians in 1901 as their first house in the UK and grew very rapidly in the 1920′s and 1930′s when a large number of houses were built in the area.

What's New

  • New School Canteen

    The canteen has been outfitted with new shop-like counters which connect to ParentPay, removing the need of cash in school and easier paying for lunches and breaktime snacks. Additionally, a board has been placed before entry to mark the day's main meals, bothe vegitarian and non-vegetarian. New frdges have been installed to store drinks and other chilled items for everyone. Parents are notified of the purchases and those who require free meals are provided £2.50 for a main meal and dessert or other ways of spending.

  • New Attendance Managment System

    We have now switched to routine termly PTM evenings which show attendance reports alongside other crucial information. Calls home were previously done individually but we have switched to bulk calls from the reception rather than form tutors. Detentions are given to late people and all rules apply during entry, exit and any other time pupils wear the school uniform. Additionally, pupils MUST sign on entry to prevent accidental computer mishaps on SIMs in which cse we contact the reception to change register details. This is a foolproof way as pupils show evidence of coming to school on that day, their signature as proof.
